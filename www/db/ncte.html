Database - Non-recursive CTE

CTE know as Common Table Expressions. It is a standard SQL feature and recently support was added in mariadb database.

You can think of them as a named result set, which can refer to itself and optionally be excuted recursively.

They are of two kind viz., Non-recursive and Recursive.

Here, we will explore Non-recursive nature of CTE. Which is simpler than the other kind ( recursive ).
